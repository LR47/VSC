Anxiety is a corrupted relationship with imagination.

It makes us imagine every bad thing.

Feeling anxious means we care deeply, we regret, and we don't want to give up.

So, we overthink everything….

And live in a world of fear and illusions.

But this is also a strength.

It helps us plan and prepare…

It's a part of us.

It's a wall we built to protect ourselves.

We sometimes think we want to disappear, but all we really want is to be found.

So be patient…

…and accept who you are.
